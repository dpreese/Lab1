R&R Photography

Reese and Ruby are twin sisters born and raised in Southern California. Growing up on the coast and with their large extended family they grew to love being social and the beach. They both grew to have a passion for photography and design throughout their life, even going as far as graduating college with bachelors degrees in both graphic design and photography. Starting their own business R&R Photagraphy in 2015, they both have years of experience behind them and always manage to capture the perfect shot of all their clients.
